## 1. Makerspace Self-Host Grant

**Any makerspace may self-host, run, modify, and adapt OSMM to operate its own
makerspace at no charge — regardless of whether that makerspace is a nonprofit,
a community group, or a for-profit business, and regardless of whether it funds
itself through paid memberships, workshop fees, or similar.**

Running your own space on your own deployment is always free. This grant covers a
makerspace using OSMM for its *own* internal operations. It does **not** permit
offering OSMM itself to third parties as a paid product or hosted service — that
is commercial use of the software and is covered by
[COMMERCIAL.md](COMMERCIAL.md).
