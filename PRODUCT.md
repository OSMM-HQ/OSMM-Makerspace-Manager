# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Community members requesting access, makerspace staff operating loans and services at a busy desk, and superadmins configuring a multi-tenant deployment. They need fast, unambiguous workflows that preserve tenant boundaries and a trustworthy chain of custody.

## Product Purpose

Space Works manages community hardware loans, 3D printing, memberships, events, bookings, and payments. Success means people can complete their task quickly while every physical handover, permission decision, and payment remains attributable and auditable.

## Brand Personality

Practical, welcoming, accountable. The interface should feel like a well-run workshop: clear enough for an occasional member, efficient enough for daily staff use, and serious about evidence without feeling bureaucratic.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic enterprise dashboards, decorative SaaS gradients, glass-heavy surfaces, oversized marketing typography, hidden security state, and novel controls where standard form or navigation patterns are clearer.

## Design Principles

- Make the next operational action obvious.
- Show scope, state, and consequences before a user commits.
- Reuse one interaction vocabulary across member, staff, and platform surfaces.
- Keep safety and traceability visible without adding ceremony to routine work.
- Prefer resilient, accessible controls over decorative novelty.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast and keyboard operation. Preserve clear focus states, semantic labels, useful errors, reduced-motion compatibility, and layouts that remain usable on phones and at browser zoom.
